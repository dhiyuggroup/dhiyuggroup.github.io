About the Journal

Our Mission

The journal is dedicated to providing a platform for publishing high-quality research in the field of Kannada studies, literature, and culture. We aim to foster intellectual exchange and bring forward innovative ideas and critical discussions.

Editorial Board Overview

Our editorial board consists of esteemed scholars and experts in Kannada studies, committed to maintaining the quality and integrity of published work. For more information about our editorial board members, please visit the Editorial Board page.
