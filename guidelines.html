Submission Guidelines

Guidelines for authors submitting articles to the Journal of Kannada Studies

General Requirements

We welcome original research articles, reviews, and critical essays related to Kannada language, literature, and culture. Please ensure your work adheres to the following guidelines before submitting.

Formatting Requirements

  • Manuscripts should be submitted in Word format (.doc or .docx).
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt size, double-spaced.
  • Title page must include the title, authorâ€™s name, and affiliation.
  • Include an abstract of up to 250 words at the beginning of the manuscript.
  • Use footnotes for citations and references in MLA style.

Peer Review Process

All submissions are subject to a double-blind peer review process. Our editorial team will review your submission, and if it meets our guidelines, it will be forwarded to reviewers. The review process typically takes 4-6 weeks.

Publication Ethics

We adhere to strict ethical guidelines. Authors must ensure that their submissions are original and have not been published or submitted elsewhere. Any conflicts of interest must be disclosed.

How to Submit

Submit your manuscript as an email attachment to submissions@journalofkannada.com.
For questions about the submission process, please contact us at the same email address.
